About Me

To finish it all off, I just want to talk a bit about my self. To start off, My name is Prabhjot Sodhi and at the age of 10, I decided I wanted to make games as a potential career or a personnel hobby. I started of with my projects wanting to make the best triple AAA game out there they would make companies like Ubisoft's games look bad. Soon to my realization that game dev isn't just punching keys and magically having something there on the screen, it was harder then I thought. Though I did not give up for the past 5 years I certainly did give up 2 weeks in because I would get frustrated and give up because it wasn't going the way I wanted. It would take me a week to recover from my lost pride and I would start again and repeat the cycle until just last year where started a project and have survived for 3 months now, which is great. That does it for my game dev journey. You can follow me through the links below to receive updates on my game project or more about me :)
