EDUCATION

Requirements

The good thing about getting started into game programming is that there is no need for a prior knowledge of anything required. It will be beneficial though to have some knowledge on programming concepts to smooth out the learning curve that is there. To give you a thumbs up, programming is not for everyone, it is diffult and most of the time your actually not coding. You need to have that mindset and tools of problem solving and sitting there hours on end researching a way/fix for your coding problems

Entering The Industry

There are two Ways of entering the Game Dev Industry. Self-Taught and through education. The best way to enter the industry is to have a bachelors or degree in some sort of game programming. When applying for a job it will require less effort on building up a portfolio then a Self-Taught person. Self-Taught is another way of entering the industry but will require much more effort as you have no degree or bachelors under your name. Your safest bet is going to be building a big portfolio as it is the only thing you can show to get the job
